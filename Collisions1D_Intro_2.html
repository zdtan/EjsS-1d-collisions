Activities

  1. There are two main modes -- "free play" and "quiz mode".
  2. In "free play", go ahead and adjust various sliders can be adjusted, starting/pausing/resetting the simulation as needed.
  3. In "quiz mode", you would need to make some calculations to determine the value of initial velocity v1 needed to achieve the desired after-impact velocity for v2. Pay attention to whether the collision is elastic/inelastic as well as the specified values of mass and initial velocity for v2.
